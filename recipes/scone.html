Classic scone recipe

Description

This is a easy and simple recipe for you to make classic English scones.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • butter
  • milk
  • an egg

Steps

  1. Grab your food processor, add in the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. After combine them, add in the cubed butter and pulse a couple times. The mixture should look something like coarse sand.
  2. Transfer the coarse flour mixture to a large bowl and in a separate small bowl whisk the milk and egg together. Reserve about 2 tablespoons of this milk/egg mixture to use for a wash later on, then pour the remaining mixture into the bowl with the flour.
  3. Pour the dough out onto a floured surface then knead it a few times, just until it's smooth. Press the dough down into a circle that's about 1 inch thick.
  4. Using a 2Â½-inch cookie cutter or the floured rim of a glass, cut round scones out of the dough. Then place the rounds onto a piece of parchment paper, making sure that the scones are touching. Once you've cut all possible circles from the dough, reshape the scraps into a 1-inch thick circle once again and cut out more.
  5. Bake them for 13 to 15 minutes in your oven preheated to 425 F.
  6. Eat them with clotted cream and jam!
